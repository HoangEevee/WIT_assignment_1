DIABETES@HOME

ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Welcome to Diabetes@Home, your personal diabetes management system. Created by Fiona Agustina, Nguyễn Quốc Hoàng, Vidhya Kudikyala, Eric Shi and Nguyen Quynh Anh, this web app will help you keep track of, and manage, your diabetes in the convenience of your own home!

Read on to hear testimonials from our users, or if you're a patient, sign in to access the app's features!

MEET HENRY

Henry, Brisbane father of two, was diagnosed with type 1 diabetes in November of 2013. After a series of close calls between 2014-2016 from Insulin spikes, Henry knew he needed a better way to track his health trends and identify early warning signs.

After joining Diabetes@Home, Henry has regained confidence in his health. With historical data and easy access to clinician advice, Henry can identify early signs of danger, and request help from the convenience of his own home.

MEET JACK

Jack, working pro-bono lawyer and father of three, was diagnosed with type 1 diabetes in August of 2016. With a very involved job and household responsibilities, it became difficult to find time for diabetes management.

After joining Diabetes@Home, Jack found support in managing his diabetes and keeping track of his health, despite the bustle of life. With the convenience of using the app from home, he now fits it comfortably into his working schedule.

MEET KARL

Karl, a third year University of Melbourne student, has lived with type 2 diabetes all his life. As someone with volatile health, Karl has always valued the support of his primary clinician in Perth, as someone who understands Karl's situation well.

After joining Diabetes@Home, and recording his health data through the app, Karl has managed to retain his patient clinician relationship. Despite the distance, with the power of historical data and a messaging system, Karl can get the support he needs.
